Installation Instructions:

  • Click on your name to open your formatted signature.
  • Copy the signature from the source document. (Use CMD+A or CTRL+E to select the signature and all the formatting from the source document, then use CMD+C or CTRL+C to copy the signature to the clipboard.)
  • On your gmail account go to Settings -> See all Settings -> General -> Signature
  • Delete your current signature and then create a new one. Do not overwrite an existing signature (see this screenshot for reference).
  • Do not add extra content. If you need to add something to your signature please send me a message and I can add that for you in the source code.
